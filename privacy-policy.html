Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

At Hygienic & Comfort Co., accessible from https://hygienicandcomfortco.shop/, protecting the privacy of our visitors and customers is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Register on our website
  • Place an order
  • Contact us for support or feedback

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • WhatsApp / Phone Number
  • Shipping & Billing Address
  • Order and transaction details

We only collect the information necessary to provide our services.

2. Legal Basis & Consent

By using our website and placing orders, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We collect personal data only when necessary to:

  • Process and deliver orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Order Fulfillment
Processing orders and arranging local delivery in Ambernath and nearby areas.

Customer Communication
Contacting you via Email or WhatsApp regarding order confirmation, delivery updates, and support.

Website Improvement
Using feedback and usage data to improve our products and website experience.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third-party marketing agencies.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to operate our website and deliver services. These services may process limited data required to perform their functions.

These include:

  • Supabase - database and authentication services
  • Website Hosting Provider - hosting, SSL security and performance
  • Payment Providers (UPI / Bank Transfer / Razorpay or similar) - secure payment processing

These providers are obligated to protect your data and use it only to provide their services.

5. Cookies & Security

Our website uses secure HTTPS/SSL encryption to protect your browsing experience.

We may use basic cookies or local storage to:

  • Maintain login sessions
  • Improve website functionality

We do not use malicious scripts, deceptive code, or auto-downloads.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Your data is stored securely using modern cloud infrastructure and encryption technologies.

7. Data Retention

We retain customer data only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Meet legal or accounting requirements

After this period, your data is securely deleted.

8. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of incorrect information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To make a request, please contact us using the details below.

9. Children's Information

Our website does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided personal data on our website, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
